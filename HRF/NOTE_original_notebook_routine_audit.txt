Basic original-notebook routine audit
=====================================

I scanned the uploaded original notebook for simple ClearAll[...] routine names and
compared them with the current development files.  The graph drawing routine
`drawGraphFromPySecDecInput` was missing and has now been restored in
HiddenRegionFinder.wl.

The following names also appear in the original notebook but are not present as
standalone public routines in the current development files:

  checkBoundaryListAgainstReference
  classifyHiddenRegionLocations
  findVariableRelabeling
  interestingBoundaryStratumQ
  internalValences

These look like older notebook-only diagnostic/comparison helpers rather than
routines needed by the current 03--08 development examples.  I have not imported
them blindly because some of their logic may have been superseded by the newer
boundary/scaling summary tables.  If any of them is needed again, it should be
ported deliberately and tested against the current data structures.
